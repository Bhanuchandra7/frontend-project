Overview

The Cardiology Department at 24/7 Hospitals specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of all heart-related conditions. Our team consists of expert cardiologists, interventional specialists, cardiac surgeons, and trained emergency professionals available round the clock.

Key Treatments & Procedures

  • Angiography & Angioplasty
  • Heart Attack Emergency Treatment (24/7)
  • Bypass Surgery (CABG)
  • Pacemaker Implantation
  • Heart Failure Management
  • Cardiac Electrophysiology
  • Valvular Heart Disease Treatment
  • Non-Invasive Cardiac Imaging

Facilities Available

  • 24/7 Cardiac Emergency Unit
  • Advanced Cath Lab
  • Dedicated Cardiac ICU
  • High-Precision Monitoring Systems
  • Holter Monitoring & Stress Tests
  • Experienced Cardiologists & Surgeons
