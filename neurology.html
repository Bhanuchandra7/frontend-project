Neurology

Advanced neurological care, emergency response, brain & nerve diagnostics, and expert neurologists available 24/7.

Overview

The Neurology Department at 24/7 Hospitals provides comprehensive care for disorders of the brain, spine, nerves, and muscles. With state-of-the-art technology and a team of highly experienced neurologists, we manage both emergency and chronic neurological conditions with precision and compassion.

Key Treatments & Procedures

  • Stroke Diagnosis & 24/7 Stroke Emergency Care
  • Epilepsy Management & EEG Monitoring
  • Brain Tumor Evaluation
  • Parkinson’s Disease Treatment
  • Multiple Sclerosis (MS) Care
  • Neuromuscular Disorder Treatment
  • Migraine & Chronic Headache Management
  • Spine & Nerve Disorders

Facilities Available

  • 24/7 Neuro Emergency Unit
  • Advanced Brain & Spine Imaging (MRI, CT)
  • Neuro ICU with Critical Care Support
  • EEG, EMG & Nerve Conduction Testing
  • Dedicated Stroke Response Team
  • Experienced Neurologists & Neurosurgeons
